About the author

Ria Om Shandilya is a Pianist, Author, Educator, and Music creator who studied music at Trinity College London and is an alumna of the Delhi School of Music. Her musical journey began at the age of 8 and led her to become a professional pianist by 16.

What sets Ria apart is her commitment to bridging a gap: the lack of professionally written sheet music for Hindi songs, compared to the abundance of detailed Western notations. She creates high-quality Indian sheet music with the same precision, elegance, and accessibility expected of Western resources.

Through her work, Ria aims to make Hindi music more approachable for learners while honoring its depth and beauty.
